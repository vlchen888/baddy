We are the Caltech official badminton club. We organize Caltech-wide weekly practices and annually tournaments, with more than 20 active members and ~100 club members.

To become an official member please fill out the CST Individual Member Registration.pdf. You may need to include a scan/picture of your health insurance card. Please give the filled form to the badminton club president Karen Chujun Lin in person or via email linchujun2011@gmail.com. All members of the Caltech community are welcome:  

  1.     Caltech Undergraduate Students

  2.     Caltech Graduate Students

  3.     Caltech Postdocs

  4.      Caltech Faculty

  5.     Caltech / JPL staff

  6.     Caltech recreation members

All levels are welcome! If you wish to receive email alerts for the club events, please sign up for the mailing list: baddyclub@caltech.edu . Go to here.

Events and weekly schedule will be broadcasted via the mailing list.
